About Free Drop Co. Ltd

Free Drop Co. Ltd (Fd) is a private enterprise run by a passionate team who believe we can contribute positively to improving our living and working spaces as well as living in a healthy life. We specialize in building construction and interior finishes in Kenya and the rest of the world. Fd has developed a powerful combination of in depth market knowledge, industry relationships, creative marketing, strong negotiating and management skills that ensure a holistic approach to the construction market, from beginning to completion. At Fd we believe that property is an extremely valuable investment that has the unique dual ability to enrich both our personal and professional lives. We walk hand in hand with our client to ensure that they get only the best of all that the market has to offer. Whether renovating, construction, exterior or interior finishes, we make your success central to our business philosophy.

Our Mission

Use the most current technology to deliver to our clients the best architectural and interior design products/services as per their needs while adhering to professional bodies, construction, health and safety laws — at the most economic, convenient, and efficient way.

Our Vision

To be the most competitive one-stop place for architectural designs, construction and interior design products and services in the local and international market.
